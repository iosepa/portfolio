About

Hello, I'm Joseph and I build websites.

Started programming in 2002. My professor said he’d give an automatic “A” to any student who could build a working calculator using VB6 in less than 15 lines of code (or something like that). I did, but he didn’t stick to his word! Surprise, surprise.

Later I built a linen tracking program to replace papers forms we'd been using at my hotel job. Then I worked for three years as a web developer. Final two years as the lead programmer; I taught and guided the other developers. At that point I thought it would be exciting to do research and work on hardware. So I became an engineer at NASA. The work was interesting, it just wasn’t as engaging. Loved the creativity, freedom and constant problem solving of web development and felt I could have a greater impact at my computer. So, here I am, ready to create, develop, and help you make the web (and world) a better place. So this is cool all that. Hire me.

"To code or not to code? Is that even a question?”
